## What is Release?

Release is SenseLab's plan for teams that need a complete, auditable record of what AI wrote and what shipped — from the moment code merges to the moment something breaks in production.

It covers the part of the SDLC that no other tool touches: the gap between merge and incident. Deployment health, runtime impact, compliance exports, and a feedback loop that makes every future review smarter.

<Info>
  Release builds on top of [AI Lineage](/ai-lineage) and [Review](/review). You get everything in both plans, plus full release-time and runtime intelligence.
</Info>

---

## The problem it solves

Most teams have no visibility into what happens after code merges:

- No connection between what shipped and what's happening in production
- When something breaks, there's no trail back to the source
- SBOM and AIBOM generation is manual, slow, or non-existent
- Compliance evidence packs have to be assembled by hand under pressure
- Problems in production never feed back into the next review cycle

Release closes the loop — automatically, at every deployment.
