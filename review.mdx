## What is Review?

Review is SenseLab's plan for engineering teams that need to understand what AI is putting into their codebase before it ships. It adds AI-aware pull request analysis, risk scoring, and AIBOM generation on top of AI Lineage — giving engineering managers the full picture at every merge.

Where AI Lineage tells you *what happened*, Review tells you *what's about to happen* — and whether you should be worried.

<Info>
  Review builds on top of [AI Lineage](/ai-lineage). You get everything in the free plan, plus PR-level intelligence.
</Info>

---

## The problem it solves

Code review was designed for humans reviewing human-written code. When AI writes the code, the dynamics break down:

- Developers are approving PRs they can't fully explain
- Risk is invisible — there's no signal about which PRs carry the most exposure
- Nobody knows which packages and dependencies the AI silently added
- AIBOM generation happens after an incident, not before a merge
- There's no connection between what's in the PR and what it will do in production

Review closes these gaps before the merge button is clicked.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Developer opens a PR">
    As soon as a pull request is opened, SenseLab begins analyzing it — attributing lines to models and developers, scanning dependencies, and computing the risk score.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review surfaces the risk">
    Reviewers see an AI-generated summary of what changed, who or what wrote it, what packages were added, and what the risk score is — directly in the PR context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AIBOM is generated">
    At review time, SenseLab generates an AIBOM for the PR. This becomes part of the evidence pack that follows the code through to release.
  </Step>
  <Step title="PR is linked to release">
    Once merged, the PR is permanently connected to its release, deployment, and any production events that follow. If something breaks, the trace goes all the way back.
  </Step>
</Steps>
